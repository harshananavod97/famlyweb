Booking Basics

Simply open the Famly Driver app, enter your pickup and drop-off location, select your preferred vehicle type, and confirm your booking. You'll be matched with a vetted driver in minutes.

Browse through our service categories like Quick Trip, Outstation Travel, or VIP Driver. Select the one that fits your needs best.

Use the in-app report button or contact our 24/7 customer support team through the app or by calling our hotline.

All rides booked through the Famly Driver app are covered by our comprehensive insurance and safety protocols.

Payments & Perks

We accept all major credit/debit cards, digital wallets, UPI payments, net banking, and cash. You can manage your payment methods in the app settings.

Go to your booking details and tap on "Payment Method" to switch between available payment options before the ride ends.

Friday Payment is our weekly payment cycle for drivers. All pending payments are processed every Friday for completed rides.
Troubleshooting & More

Try restarting the app, checking your internet connection, and ensuring location services are enabled. If issues persist, contact our 24/7 support team.

Common issues include GPS accuracy, payment processing delays, and app loading. Most can be resolved by updating the app to the latest version.
